Welcome to BEC

Bapatla Engineering College is one of the seven educational institutions sponsored by the Bapatla Education Society, was established in 1981 with a vision to impart quality technical education and is affiliated to Acharya Nagarjuna University. The College is a little away from the din and bustle of Bapatla, a town with a historic and hoary past, about 75 Km. south of Vijayawada on Chennai-Vijayawada rail route. The college offers B.Tech. Programmes in 9 branches of Engineering- Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, CSE(Cyber Security), CSE(Data Science), CSE(AIML), Electronics & Communication Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Information Technology and Mechanical Engineering. CE, CSE, ME & EEE Programmes NBA accredited FOUR times. ECE programme NBA accrediated THREE times. IT programme NBA accrediated ONCE.
